- WebUOT masks are tracked-target bounding-box masks, not complete fish instance masks.
- AQUALOC sanity uses clean all-static clips and synthetic fish/snow injected on real AQUALOC harbor backgrounds; report as external-background stress sanity, not natural dynamic AQUALOC SOTA.
